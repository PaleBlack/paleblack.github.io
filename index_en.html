Biography

It all began with two friends from the Emmental who shared a passion for metal

In 2009 the two friends, Thomas and Sebastian decided to start a metal band. With the most minimalis advert ever "death metal band seeks drummer", they caught Dani's attention in the summer of 2011. It was soon evident that they shared not only the same sense of humor, but also the same musical vision. The three threw out all the old songs, and got busy creating new ones. Once they had the basics, they started looking for a bass guitarist and a singer. During this time they met many musicians, but only found people to fill the gap in the winter of 2013. When they met Miriam, her solo performance of Janice Joplin's " Mercedes Benz" convinced them immediately. She soon learned how to growl, and with Bruno she also introduced the perfect Bass player into the band. The band was finally complete. They practiced hard in the Emmental, and half a year later, Pale Black was on stage for the first time. Since then they have enjoyed doing more gigs and have also written more songs. In 2016 the band shot the music video to the song Brainfuck and secured the financing of their first album via crowdfunding. It was recorded in 2017 and published, together with the music video, in 2018. Shortly after recording his parts Sebastian left the band and the country and went on a trip to the far east. Luckily with Julian a worthy replacement was found, the two guitars make sure you get your blast.

Members

These five cheerful souls rarely ever take anything serious, except for their music

Vocals: Miriam

The only woman in the band can hold her own against the guys thanks to her powerful voice. She writes the song text which are critical of society and provide the desired depth to Pale Black songs.

Guitar: Thomas

He, together with Julian, is responsible for the guitar wall that Pale Black blasts at you. His huge passion for metal is the driving force behind the band.

Bass: Bruno

As soon as he gets hold of a bass guitar, the quiet, calm Bruno changes into an animal. His tempo stability helps Dani stay in time and he thrills the audiences with his melodies on the frettless bass.

Guitar: Julian

Always on the search for the perfect sound, he is testing various amp and guitar combinations. His head seems to be full of melodies and strange accords, he is barely stoppable once his hands touch a guitar.

Drums: Dani

He forms the rhythm section together with Bruno. He causes some confusion with his visions of the order of beats and polyrhythms.

Former members

Guitar until 2017: Sebastian

Together with Thomas he founded Pale Black and heavily influenced the band and its sound. After recording his parts for the first album, he left Switzerland to travel overland to India.
